Our Suite of Tools

DNA Analyzer

The DNA Analyzer is your command-line companion for a variety of network diagnostic tasks. With its ability to perform Root Cause Analysis, log examination, and data consistency checks across Postgres database tables, this tool is essential for maintaining a healthy network environment.

DNA ISE Integration Script

Ensure accurate troubleshooting by capturing the most recent data with the DNA ISE Integration Script. Designed to perform read-only actions, this script aids in collecting critical information required for a detailed Root Cause Analysis (RCA). Get the insights you need without affecting your system's integrity.

SDA Digger

Dive deep into your SDA Fabric with SDA Digger, a comprehensive tool that aids in the diagnosis of your Software Defined Access Fabric. From consistency checks to pattern recognition, SDA Digger alerts you to inconsistencies and known issues within your network, simplifying the troubleshooting process.
